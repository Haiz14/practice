HEALTH BENEFITS OF APPLES

Heart Health

Apples contain soluble fiber which can help lower blood cholesterol levels. The polyphenols in apples are linked to lower blood pressure.

Gut Health

Apples contain pectin, a type of fiber that acts as a prebiotic, feeding the good bacteria in your gut for improved digestion.

Weight Management

The fiber and water content in apples helps you feel full. Studies show eating apples can reduce calorie intake and support weight loss.
